About FICMR 2026

The FISAT Business School International Conference on Management Research (FICMR) 2026 is a premier global forum organized by FISAT Business School focusing on sustainability, innovation, and management research.

With an overarching theme of Redefining Management Through Sustainability, this conference fosters dialogue, collaborative research, and the cross-pollination of ideas designed to introduce sustainable practices across business sectors.

Join us in Kerala to exchange transformative concepts, share insightful case studies, and network with leading minds driven by ecological and socioeconomic improvement.

Call for Papers

We invite original research papers, conceptual papers, and insightful case studies that align with the conference theme and related tracks.
